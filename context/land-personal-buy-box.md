# Brian's Personal Land Buy Box

Brian's personal filter on the Bartow County land pool. This sits on top of the
market-level screening in `references/land-wholesale-buy-box.md` — the market
qualifies the *zip*, this box qualifies an *individual parcel*.

## Criteria

| Criteria | Requirement |
|---|---|
| **Location** | Cartersville, Cassville, White, or Emerson (Bartow County) |
| **Size** | 2+ acres |
| **Topography** | Flat, buildable land |

## Zip mapping (for Land Sellers "Buy Box" column)

| City | Zip(s) |
|---|---|
| Cartersville | 30120, 30121 |
| Cassville | routes through 30120 |
| White | 30184 |
| Emerson | 30137 |

## Notes

- **Flat/buildable** cannot be assessed from county parcel data — requires a ground
  check or satellite review. The `✓ Match` flag in Land Sellers covers location + acres
  only. Confirm topography before contracting (use `/land-deal` deal-killer checklist).
- This is Brian's current working filter and may tighten as builder buy boxes come in
  from `/land-builders` calls.
