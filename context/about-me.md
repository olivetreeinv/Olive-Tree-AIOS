# About Brian Norton

## Who I Am

I'm Brian Norton, CEO of Olive Tree Investments. I built the company on three principles: transparency, trust, and collaboration. The name reflects the mission — abundance and growth, not just for us but for the communities we invest in. I'm focused on multifamily real estate in Georgia and beyond, working to simplify the investment process and bring the right people into deals that create real value.

## My Biggest Weekly Drag

Finding and underwriting a deal. The sourcing process — identifying properties, running the numbers, stress-testing assumptions — is the most time-intensive recurring task in the business. Every week I'm not under contract, I'm still grinding through this. This is the highest-leverage area for AI to help.
