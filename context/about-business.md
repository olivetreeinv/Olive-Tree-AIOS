# About Olive Tree Investments

## What We Do

Olive Tree Investments is a multifamily real estate syndication firm operating in Georgia and beyond. We find apartment buildings (15–50+ doors), partner with operators and experts, execute value-add renovations, improve operations, raise rents to market, and return profits to our investors. Deal timeline is typically 4–6 years, with a refinance event around year 4 and a sale at year 6.

## Revenue Model

Investors receive preferred returns (up to 6% pref) before the GP takes any profit. After the pref is met, profits split 70% LP / 30% GP. Average projected ROI: 18.21% annually. Investors who stay through refi + sale target a 2.09x equity multiple. Revenue lands in Bluevine business bank accounts and is tracked in QuickBooks.

## Our Investors — 4 Types

- **Individuals** — Min. $25K, accredited or non-accredited. We match them to deals that fit their goals.
- **High Net Worth** — Individualized process; properties matched to their specific risk, cash flow, and time horizon.
- **Family Offices** — Value-add real estate as a diversification play with long-term appreciation potential.
- **Institutions** — Preferred equity positions; matched to appropriate opportunities.
