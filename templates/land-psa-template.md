# Land Purchase and Sale Agreement — Template

> ⚠️ ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED before use. This is a starting-point draft only.
> Have a Georgia real estate attorney review this document before sending to any
> seller. Verify GA wholesaling/assignment disclosure requirements with counsel.
> Nothing in this document constitutes legal advice.

Used by `/land-contract` (`scripts/land_contract.py`). Placeholders in `{{...}}`
are filled per parcel. The assignability clause and feasibility contingency are
the two non-negotiable clauses for land wholesaling — do not remove them.

---

**PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT**
**(Vacant Land — Assignable)**

**Date:** {{DATE}}

**Buyer:** {{BUYER_NAME}}, and/or Assigns
**Seller:** {{SELLER_NAME}}
**Seller Mailing Address:** {{SELLER_MAILING_ADDRESS}}

---

**1. PROPERTY**

Seller agrees to sell and Buyer agrees to purchase the following real property
("Property"):

- **Parcel ID / Tax Map Number:** {{PARCEL_ID}}
- **Situs Address / Description:** {{SITUS_ADDRESS}}
- **County:** {{COUNTY}}, Georgia
- **Approximate Acreage:** {{ACRES}} acres

---

**2. PURCHASE PRICE AND TERMS**

Purchase Price: **{{PURCHASE_PRICE}}**

- All cash at closing.
- Buyer pays all closing costs. Purchase Price is net to Seller.
- No realtor fees or commissions are deducted from the Purchase Price.

**Earnest Money Deposit (EMD):** {{EMD}}
- EMD is due at or before closing, **not** at signing.
- If Buyer terminates during the Feasibility Period (Section 4), no EMD is owed.

---

**3. CLOSING**

Closing shall occur on or before **{{CLOSING_DATE}}** (approximately {{CLOSE_DAYS}}
days from the Effective Date), at a title company or closing attorney of Buyer's
choice. Closing may be conducted remotely; Seller may sign via overnight mail,
e-sign, or notary at Seller's location.

The Effective Date is the date the last party signs this Agreement.

---

**4. FEASIBILITY CONTINGENCY**

Buyer shall have until closing (the "Feasibility Period") to inspect the Property
and conduct any due diligence Buyer deems necessary, including but not limited to:
soil tests, wetland assessments, flood zone verification, title search, survey,
utility availability, zoning review, and environmental assessment.

If, in Buyer's sole discretion, the Property is not acceptable for any reason,
Buyer may terminate this Agreement by written notice to Seller at any time
before the Closing Date. Upon termination under this Section, **no EMD is owed**
and neither party shall have further obligations to the other.

---

**5. ASSIGNABILITY** *(Non-negotiable — the core of the wholesaling model)*

Buyer may assign this Agreement, or Buyer's rights hereunder, to any third party
("Assignee") without Seller's consent. Upon assignment, Buyer shall be released
from all further obligations under this Agreement, and Assignee shall assume all
of Buyer's obligations. Seller agrees to close with Assignee on the same terms.

The phrase "and/or Assigns" in the Buyer designation above is sufficient notice
of this right.

---

**6. TITLE**

Seller shall convey marketable title to the Property by Warranty Deed at closing,
free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, and assessments except current-year
property taxes (prorated to the Closing Date) and easements or restrictions of
record that do not materially impair the use of the Property as vacant land.

If title is not marketable and cannot be cured within 14 days of written notice,
Buyer may terminate this Agreement with no further obligations.

---

**7. CONDITION OF PROPERTY**

Property is sold AS IS. Seller makes no warranties as to the condition, zoning,
permitted use, availability of utilities, absence of hazardous materials, or
suitability for any particular purpose. Buyer's right to inspect is set forth
in Section 4.

---

**8. DEFAULT**

If Seller defaults, Buyer may: (a) terminate this Agreement, or (b) seek specific
performance. If Buyer defaults (other than by exercising the termination right in
Section 4), Seller's sole remedy is the EMD (if paid).

---

**9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT**

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. No verbal
representations or prior negotiations are binding unless incorporated herein.
This Agreement may be amended only by written instrument signed by both parties.

---

**10. GOVERNING LAW**

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Georgia. Venue for
any dispute shall be {{COUNTY}}, Georgia.
