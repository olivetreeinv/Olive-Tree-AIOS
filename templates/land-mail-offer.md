# Land Mass-Offer Mail Template

Used by `/land-mail` (`scripts/land_mail.py`) to merge one letter per parcel from
the Land Sellers tab. Placeholders in `{{...}}` are filled per parcel. Keep it to
one page. Nothing sends automatically — Brian reviews, prints, and mails.

> ⚠️ The offer references an assignable cash purchase. Pair with the attorney-
> reviewed `templates/land-psa-template.md` as the enclosed contract.

---

{{DATE}}

{{OWNER_NAME}}
{{OWNER_MAILING_ADDRESS}}
{{OWNER_CITY}}, {{OWNER_STATE}} {{OWNER_ZIP}}

Re: Your property at {{SITUS_ADDRESS}}, {{COUNTY}} ({{ACRES}} acres) — Parcel {{PARCEL_ID}}

Dear {{OWNER_NAME}},

My name is Brian Norton with Olive Tree Investments. I buy vacant land in
{{COUNTY}} and I'd like to make you a straightforward cash offer on the parcel
above that you own.

**My offer: {{OFFER}} cash**, with these terms:
- I pay **all closing costs** — the offer is net to you.
- **No realtor fees** and no commissions.
- I can **close in 2–3 weeks** through a title company; you sign remotely.

I work with builders and developers in the area, so I can move quickly and with
certainty. If the property has sat unused while the tax bill climbs, this is a
clean way to turn it into cash without listing it, waiting months, or paying 8–10%
to an agent.

If {{OFFER}} works for you, sign the enclosed agreement and return it in the
envelope provided, or just call or text me and we'll handle the rest. If the
number is off, call me anyway — I'd rather hear your number than lose the chance.

Warm regards,

Brian Norton
Olive Tree Investments
[PHONE] · [EMAIL]

*P.S. If you own other vacant parcels — here or elsewhere — I'll make offers on
those too. And if you refer a friend or family member whose land I end up buying,
I'll send you $1,000 at closing.*
