# Assignment of Purchase and Sale Agreement — Template

> ⚠️ ATTORNEY REVIEW REQUIRED before use. This is a starting-point draft only.
> Have a Georgia real estate attorney review this document. Confirm assignment
> fee disclosure requirements in your state/county.

Used by `/land-contract` (`scripts/land_contract.py`). Placeholders in `{{...}}`
are filled per deal. This document transfers the PSA (seller contract) to the
builder/end-buyer in exchange for the assignment fee.

---

**ASSIGNMENT OF PURCHASE AND SALE AGREEMENT**

**Date:** {{DATE}}

**Assignor (Wholesaler):** {{BUYER_NAME}} ("Assignor")
**Assignee (Builder/End-Buyer):** {{ASSIGNEE_NAME}} ("Assignee")

---

**1. RECITALS**

Assignor is the Buyer under that certain Purchase and Sale Agreement dated
{{PSA_DATE}} (the "PSA"), for the purchase of certain real property identified
as Parcel {{PARCEL_ID}}, commonly known as {{SITUS_ADDRESS}}, {{COUNTY}},
Georgia, approximately {{ACRES}} acres (the "Property"), from {{SELLER_NAME}}
("Seller") at a contract price of {{CONTRACT_PRICE}}.

---

**2. ASSIGNMENT**

Assignor hereby assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee all of Assignor's
right, title, and interest in and to the PSA, effective as of the date above.
Assignee accepts the assignment and assumes all of Assignor's obligations under
the PSA from and after the date of this Assignment.

Assignor is hereby released from all further obligations under the PSA.

---

**3. ASSIGNMENT FEE**

In consideration of this Assignment, Assignee shall pay Assignor an assignment
fee of **{{ASSIGNMENT_FEE}}** (the "Assignment Fee"), due and payable at closing
of the Property, to be collected by the closing attorney/title company and
disbursed to Assignor at closing.

**Assignment Price (Assignee's total cost):** {{ASSIGNMENT_PRICE}}
(Contract Price {{CONTRACT_PRICE}} + Assignment Fee {{ASSIGNMENT_FEE}})

---

**4. EARNEST MONEY**

Assignee shall deposit **{{ASSIGNEE_EMD}}** as earnest money with the closing
attorney/title company within {{EMD_DAYS}} days of the date of this Assignment.
The original PSA EMD (if any) shall be credited to Assignee at closing.

---

**5. SELLER NOTIFICATION**

Assignee acknowledges that Seller will be notified of this Assignment at or
before closing, per the PSA's assignment provision. Assignee agrees to cooperate
with any reasonable notification requirements.

---

**6. REPRESENTATIONS**

Assignor represents that: (a) the PSA is in full force and effect; (b) Assignor
has not modified the PSA without Assignee's consent; and (c) Assignor has the
right to assign the PSA.

Assignee represents that: (a) Assignee has reviewed the PSA; and (b) Assignee
is acquiring the Property in its AS IS condition per the PSA.

---

**7. ENTIRE AGREEMENT**

This Assignment, together with the PSA (attached as Exhibit A), constitutes the
entire agreement between Assignor and Assignee with respect to the assignment.

---

**8. GOVERNING LAW**

This Assignment shall be governed by the laws of the State of Georgia.
