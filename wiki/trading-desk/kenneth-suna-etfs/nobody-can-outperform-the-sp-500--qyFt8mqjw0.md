## Summary
Kenneth Suna discusses his personal stock investment performance since 2022, arguing that individual investors can outperform the S&P 500 through stock picking and conviction investing. The video contains no options trading strategies, premium collection tactics, or risk management principles relevant to Brian Norton's Premium Desk covered call and cash-secured put wheel strategy.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Individual stock selection requires time and research effort to execute effectively
2. High-conviction positions should be averaged into over multiple years rather than bought once
3. Dollar-cost averaging into winning positions (Nvidia, Meta, Palantir) across multiple years outperforms static allocation
4. Accepting losses on some positions (Cava, Hims, Meta) is part of a diversified stock-picking approach
5. Speculative positions (QBTS, Joby Aviation) are included alongside core holdings in a mixed portfolio
