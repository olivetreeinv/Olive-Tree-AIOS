## Summary
Kenneth Suna presents a hierarchical approach to building an investment portfolio for buy-and-hold investors, recommending S&P 500 index funds as the foundation, followed by tech ETFs, dividend ETFs, and REITs. The video emphasizes researching individual stocks of companies you know, use, and love, using historical examples (Chipotle, Apple, Meta, ELF) to illustrate long-term wealth creation potential.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Start with S&P 500 index fund as primary investment
2. Add tech-based ETF as second investment
3. Add dividend-based ETF as third investment
4. Add REIT (real estate investment trust) as fourth investment
5. Research individual stocks only from companies you know, use, and love
6. Use fundamental analysis resources and stock comparison templates before investing
7. Many REITs pay monthly dividends
