## Summary
Kenneth Suna discusses dividend-paying ETFs (VTG, DVY, DWT, SCHD) as a passive investing strategy for early retirement, covering their expense ratios, dividend yields, and 5-year dividend growth rates. This is fundamentally a buy-and-hold dividend investing approach unrelated to active options trading or premium generation.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Vanguard Dividend Appreciation (VTG): $156/share, 0.06% expense ratio, 1.93% dividend yield, 10% 5-year dividend growth, A+ safety grade
2. iShares Core Dividend (DVY): $50/share, 0.08% expense ratio, 2.4% dividend yield, 10.32% 5-year dividend growth, A safety grade
3. Wisdom Tree Dividend (DWT): $63/share, 0.28% expense ratio, 2.07% dividend yield, 12% 5-year dividend growth, B+ safety grade
4. Schwab US Dividend (SCHD): $70/share, 0.06% expense ratio, 3.73% dividend yield, 15.5% 5-year dividend growth, A+ safety grade
