## Summary
Kenneth Suna compares S&P 500 index funds (VO, SPY, SPLG) to demonstrate that despite different share prices, all three ETFs deliver approximately the same 1.5% dividend yield. This is educational content about index fund selection, not directly applicable to Brian Norton's Premium Desk options strategy.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. All major S&P 500 ETFs (VO, SPY, SPLG) deliver roughly 1.5% dividend yield regardless of share price
2. Share price differences are offset by dividend per share differences—buying 850 shares of SPLG at $53 vs. 100 shares of SPY at $450 with same capital yields equivalent returns (~$671-$650 annual dividend on $45,000)
