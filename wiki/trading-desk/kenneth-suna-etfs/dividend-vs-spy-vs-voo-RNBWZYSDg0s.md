## Summary
Kenneth compares dividend yields across SPY, VOO, and SPLG ETFs to show that despite SPLG's lower share price, all three offer approximately the same 1.5% dividend yield when accounting for share count, making the choice economically neutral rather than advantageous.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. All three major broad-market ETFs (SPY, VOO, SPLG) yield approximately 1.5% annually
2. Share price alone does not determine dividend value; total capital invested and dividend per share must be considered together
3. $45,000 invested in VOO (100 shares) yields ~$620/year vs. SPLG (850 shares) yields ~$671/year—functionally equivalent
