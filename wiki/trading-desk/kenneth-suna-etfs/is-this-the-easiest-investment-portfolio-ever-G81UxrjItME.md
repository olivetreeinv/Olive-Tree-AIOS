## Summary
Kenneth Suna provides long-term retirement investment advice focused on Roth IRA and S&P 500 index fund allocation, with a 4% withdrawal rule in retirement. The content emphasizes buy-and-hold investing over 30+ years and gradual bond allocation during retirement, with no options strategies or active trading discussed.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Roth IRA contribution limit is currently $7,000/year (potentially increasing to $7,500)
2. Cannot withdraw from Roth IRA until age 59.5
3. At retirement, sell approximately 10% per year over 4-5 years and allocate into bond ETFs for safety
4. Target portfolio allocation in retirement: 60% S&P 500 index fund, 40% bond ETF
5. Use the 4% withdrawal rule for retirement income planning
6. Maintain consistent investing for 30 years regardless of market headlines or noise
