## Summary
Kenneth Suna discusses long-term custodial account investing for children using broad-based ETFs (SPLG, SCHD, QQQ, REALTY) rather than active options strategies. This is fundamentally about buy-and-hold index investing over 15-40 year horizons, not options selling or premium generation.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. SPLG (S&P 500 ETF): 167% return over 10 years, 1.53% dividend, 0.02% expense ratio
2. S&P 500 average return ~10% annually; $300/month for 15 years grows to $121k by age 18
3. SCHD (Schwab US Dividend ETF): 123% return, 3.56% dividend, 14% 5-year dividend growth
4. QQQ (Nasdaq 100): 380% return in 10 years, 49% technology allocation
5. REALTY (Realty Income): 5.44% dividend, pays monthly, 46% 10-year return
6. Custodial accounts recommended for tax-advantaged long-term wealth building for minors
