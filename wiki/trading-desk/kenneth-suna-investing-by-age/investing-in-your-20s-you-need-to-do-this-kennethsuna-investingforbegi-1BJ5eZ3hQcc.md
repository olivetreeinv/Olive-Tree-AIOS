## Summary
Kenneth Suna provides beginner investing advice focused on opening a Roth IRA and building a long-term portfolio with index funds and ETFs (S&P 500, tech, dividend, and REIT funds). This content is foundational wealth-building advice rather than options trading or premium-selling strategies relevant to Brian Norton's Premium Desk operations.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Max out Roth IRA contribution at $7,000 for 2024
2. Start with S&P 500 Index Fund as core holding
3. Vanguard Information Technology ETF is 99% tech exposure, up 426% in last decade
4. Schwab dividend ETF has A+ dividend safety grade with 4.14% dividend growth over 5 years
5. Real estate investment trust (REIT) paying 5.63% annual yield with 25 years of consecutive dividend growth
6. REIT pays monthly dividends of 26 cents per share
7. Open brokerage accounts at platforms like Schwab or Fidelity
