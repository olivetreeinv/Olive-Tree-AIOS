## Summary
Kenneth Suna discusses long-term retirement investing strategy for young adults using Roth IRA and individual brokerage accounts with index funds and tech ETFs. This is general wealth-building advice unrelated to options trading, premium selling, or active portfolio management.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Open both Roth IRA (tax-free growth until age 59.5) and individual brokerage account as a young adult
2. Allocate to technology funds (QQQ up 517%, VGT up 525%, XLK up 507% over 10 years) in Roth IRA for tax-free gains
3. Allocate S&P 500 index funds (up 198% over 10 years) in individual account for flexibility
4. Individual account allows early access before retirement with taxes owed on profits
5. Buy major tech holdings: Microsoft, Apple, Amazon, Meta, Alphabet, Tesla
6. Long-term index investing can enable retirement by age 45 before peers
