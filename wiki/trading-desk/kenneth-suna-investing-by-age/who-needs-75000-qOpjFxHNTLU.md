## Summary
Kenneth Suna discusses retirement income needs and expenses, breaking down a $75,000 annual budget across healthcare, food, transportation, housing, travel, entertainment, and other costs. The content is a financial planning discussion unrelated to options trading, covered calls, cash-secured puts, or any premium-selling strategies relevant to Brian Norton's Premium Desk.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Healthcare costs $12-18k/year in retirement
2. Food expenses $8-12k/year for retired couple
3. Transportation (1 car) runs $6-10k/year
4. Housing expenses (taxes, insurance, utilities, fees) $6-10k/year
5. Annual travel budget $10-15k for one vacation
6. Entertainment (concerts, streaming, subscriptions) $4-8k/year
7. Clothing and incidentals $2-4k/year
8. Inflation adjusts purchasing power over 20-30 years ($40k today could be $80k in future dollars)
