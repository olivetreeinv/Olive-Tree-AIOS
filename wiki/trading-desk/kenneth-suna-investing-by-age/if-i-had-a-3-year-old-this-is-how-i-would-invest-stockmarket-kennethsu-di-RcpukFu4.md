## Summary
Kenneth Suna discusses long-term buy-and-hold ETF investing strategy for a hypothetical 3-year-old child using custodial accounts. The video focuses on passive index funds (SPLG, SCHD, QQQ, REALTY) with 15-43 year time horizons and dividend accumulation, not options trading or income generation strategies relevant to Brian's Premium Desk operations.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. SPLG (S&P 500 ETF) historical return: 167% in 10 years, 1.53% dividend, 0.02% expense ratio
2. Average S&P 500 return target: ~10% annually
3. $300/month invested for 15 years into S&P 500 = $121,000 by age 18; continues to $612,900 by age 33; $1.6M by age 43
4. SCHD (Schwab Dividend ETF): 123% in 10 years, 3.56% dividend, 14% five-year dividend growth, 11 years consecutive dividend growth
5. QQQ (Nasdaq-100): 380% in 10 years, 49% technology holdings
6. REALTY (Realty Income REIT): 46% in 10 years, 5.44% dividend paid monthly
