## Summary
Kenneth discusses portfolio allocation strategy across different age groups and the importance of rebalancing toward bonds as you approach retirement. The content focuses on long-term buy-and-hold investing principles rather than options trading or income generation strategies relevant to Brian's Premium Desk operation.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Ages 20-40: predominantly tech ETF, S&P 500, and dividend positions
2. At age 40: reduce tech from 50% to 40%, add 10% bonds
3. At age 50: further reduce tech and S&P allocation, increase dividend and bond positions
4. Follow the Wall Street adage of investing your age in bonds, though it may not apply to younger investors aged 20-40
5. Market downturns can take years to recover; younger investors can average in with larger capital to capitalize on sell-offs
