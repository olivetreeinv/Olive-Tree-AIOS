## Summary
Kenneth Suna recommends a buy-and-hold portfolio construction approach using ETFs and REITs (S&P 500, dividend ETF, tech ETF, real estate trust) for passive long-term wealth building. This is fundamentally misaligned with Brian Norton's Premium Desk strategy of active options selling (covered calls and cash-secured puts) targeting $1,250/month in premium income.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. S&P 500 Index Fund (SPY) up 22% in last 12 months with very low fees
2. Schwab US Dividend ETF up 114% over last decade with 133% dividend growth over 5 years
3. Tech ETF (XLK) up 463% over last 10 years with extremely low fees
4. REALTY Income (O) up 24% in last 10 years with monthly dividend payments and no management fees
5. Diversification strategy: start with one core holding, progressively add dividend income, growth exposure, and real estate
