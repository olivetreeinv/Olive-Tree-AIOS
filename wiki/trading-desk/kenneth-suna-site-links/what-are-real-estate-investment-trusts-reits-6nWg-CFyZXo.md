## Summary
Kenneth Suna discusses various Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) and their dividend yields, including Realty Income, American Tower, CubeSmart, and Welltower. The video focuses on REIT fundamentals, historical performance, and dividend structures rather than options trading strategies.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Realty Income (O) pays 5.89% dividend or 26 cents monthly
2. American Tower up 148% in 10 years with 3.37% quarterly dividend (~$1.70/quarter)
3. CubeSmart pays 4.68% dividend quarterly (~51 cents)
4. Welltower pays 2.63% dividend quarterly (~61 cents)
5. REITs provide diversified real estate exposure beyond office space (retail, cell towers, data centers, self-storage, healthcare)
