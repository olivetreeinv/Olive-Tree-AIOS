## Summary
Kenneth Suna provides a beginner's guide to opening a brokerage account and selecting ETFs (SPLG, SCHD, XLK) plus REITs for a passive four-fund portfolio approach. The content focuses on long-term buy-and-hold investing for retirement accounts rather than active options trading strategies.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Roth IRA max contribution is $7,000/year
2. S&P 500 Index Funds have ~10% average annual return
3. Dividend ETFs like SCHD have ~12% dividend growth over 5 years
4. XLKY (tech ETF) returned 472% over last decade
5. REIT expense ratios under 1% are considered low
6. Realty Income (REIT) has 26 consecutive years of dividend increases at 5.67% yield
7. Use four-fund approach: S&P 500, dividend ETF, tech ETF, and REIT
