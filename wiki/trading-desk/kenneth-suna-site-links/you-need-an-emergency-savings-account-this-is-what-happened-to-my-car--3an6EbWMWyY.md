2. Unexpected expenses can compound quickly ($15 tip + $80 gas + $300 mobile service + $585 mechanic repair)
3. Emergency savings should cover at least 1-3 months of living expenses to avoid financial stress
4. Without emergency fund, households are vulnerable to 'financial ruin' from single incidents
