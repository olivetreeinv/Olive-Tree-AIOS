## Summary
Kenneth Suna demonstrates his covered call strategy on a $50k+ portfolio, generating $600-$1,400 weekly (~$50-$70k annually) with minimal time investment. He outlines his Sunday night research phase (20-30 min) followed by Monday market-open execution (30 min), emphasizing the risk-reward tradeoff of the strategy.

## Key Takeaways
1. Research phase on Sunday night takes 20-30 minutes before market open
2. Monday morning execution at 9:30am market open takes approximately 30 minutes
3. Consistent average weekly income of $1,000 from covered calls
4. Total weekly time commitment under one hour for $50-$70k annualized income
5. Kenneth generates $600-$1,400 per week selling covered calls
6. Strategy requires re-evaluating positions based on news and upcoming earnings announcements
