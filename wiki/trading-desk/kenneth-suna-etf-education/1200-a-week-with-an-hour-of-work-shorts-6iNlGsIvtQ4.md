## Summary
Kenneth Suna demonstrates a covered call strategy generating $600-$1,400 weekly ($50k-$70k annually) with approximately one hour of work per week: 20-30 minutes Sunday research, 30 minutes Monday morning execution including share purchases and call sales.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Generate $600-$1,400 per week with covered calls on consistent basis
2. Sunday night research takes 20-30 minutes to compile list and evaluate premiums
3. Monday morning market open (9:30 AM) is execution window for refined pricing and news evaluation
4. Full execution cycle (buying shares + selling covered calls) takes ~30 minutes on Monday
5. Can generate $50k-$70k annually with under one hour weekly time commitment
6. Evaluate upcoming earnings dates before trade entry to avoid adverse moves
7. Monitor updated options premiums and pricing Monday morning before executing full position
