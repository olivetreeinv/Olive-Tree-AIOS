## Summary
Kenneth Suna documents a covered call series where he aims to make $180/week per position by selling calls on stocks like HIMS (~$50/share). He demonstrates that the strategy is scalable across multiple positions with different capital amounts, generating ~$1,300+ weekly across a $27k portfolio, debunking TikTok claims that only high-volatility stocks like Tesla ($900/week) make this strategy viable.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target $180/week per covered call on HIMS stock (~$50/share cost)
2. $5,000 capital generates ~$80-100/week in premium
3. Position sizes vary: $5k=$80, $9,950=$322, $5,600=$240, $3,200=$146, $3,900=$245 weekly
4. Don't need $27k total; can start with 1-3 positions if capital-constrained
5. High-volatility stocks like Tesla require $100+ shares (expensive entry); focus on more accessible stocks with decent premiums instead
6. Weekly adjustments planned as positions get assigned or expire
7. Realistic expectation: $80-322/week per position depending on capital deployed
