## Summary
Kenneth demonstrates a covered call strategy on Ford stock held since 2008, selling 6 contracts at the $16 strike when implied volatility was elevated after a stock price surge. He earned $302 in premium while retaining shares, planning to repeat the process on any pullbacks until eventual assignment.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Sell covered calls when IV is elevated after stock momentum (Ford jumped $12→$16)
2. Strike selection: sell at-the-money or slightly out-of-the-money strikes ($16 strike when stock at $16)
3. Monitor premium decay week-to-week: $302 in week 1 vs only $120 potential in week 2 after momentum fizzled
4. Covered calls allow profit on premium even if stock breaks even or declines slightly
5. Plan for eventual assignment but use interim periods to layer additional premium sales
6. 6 contracts on several-hundred-share position provides reasonable capital efficiency
