## Summary
Kenneth Suna demonstrates how to generate significant weekly premium income by selling covered calls on high-priced stocks, using real examples ranging from $256/week (Nvidia) to $9,500/month (AutoZone). The strategy requires owning 100 shares per contract and targets wealthy investors building generational wealth through consistent weekly or monthly premium collection.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Covered calls require 100 shares minimum per contract
2. Weekly expirations allow premium collection every single week
3. High-priced stocks generate substantially larger premiums: Nvidia $256, Microsoft $430, Tesla $875, Broadcom $1,400, Booking Holdings $4,900/week
4. AutoZone generates ~$9,500 monthly but only offers monthly contracts (not weekly)
5. Strike prices are typically set near current stock price (e.g., Nvidia at $187.50 strike for $18,500 stock cost)
