## Summary
Kenneth documents his weekly covered call selling performance across multiple stocks (HIMS, OLO, JOBY, Nphase), achieving $1,133 in premiums on a $27,455 deployed capital for a 4.13% weekly return. He emphasizes accepting 'good enough' returns rather than chasing maximum profits, using a diversified single-option approach across 4-5 positions rather than the $50k Premium Desk model.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target strikes just above purchase price to ensure assignment (e.g., bought HIMS at $50.38, sold $50-51 calls)
2. Aim for 4-6.2% weekly premium returns on covered calls as acceptable performance
3. Accept assignment and redeploy capital weekly rather than holding for larger moves
4. Extend expiration to next week (7+ days) when premiums are exceptionally high (Nphase example at 6.2%)
5. Buying after strong rallies is high-risk but acceptable if premium income justifies the risk
6. Deploy capital across multiple positions simultaneously (4-5 stocks in one week) rather than concentrating on single stock
7. Philosophy: 'Your goal isn't to make all the money, just some of it' - recognize good enough returns to avoid regret
