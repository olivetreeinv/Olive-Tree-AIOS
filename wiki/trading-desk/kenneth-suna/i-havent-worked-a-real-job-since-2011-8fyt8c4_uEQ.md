## Summary
Kenneth teaches a disciplined covered call strategy executed in ~1 hour/week: scout downtrending stocks on Sunday evening, evaluate premiums Monday morning at market open (9:30am), target high-beta volatile stocks for 2-7% weekly premiums, and aim for assignment as the primary goal. He emphasizes buying dips rather than overbought highs and accepting smaller but consistent gains over maximum returns.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Buy stocks in downtrends (not at all-time highs or overbought levels) to increase probability of stock recovery and assignment
2. Target high-beta stocks that are more volatile than overall market to generate higher premiums (3-7% per contract)
3. Sunday evening: spend 30 minutes creating watchlist of 15-20 stocks in downtrends that could rebound
4. Monday at 9:30am market open: spend 10-15 minutes reviewing premiums for all positions and calculating per-contract gains
5. Adjust risk tolerance by comparing premium opportunities: accept 2-3% gains to avoid earnings risk, or take 7% if risk parameters allow
6. Sell covered calls at strike prices slightly above purchase price (e.g., buy at $25, sell $26 strike) accepting capped upside for consistent premium income
7. Goal is not maximum profit but consistent, achievable gains paired with high assignment probability
