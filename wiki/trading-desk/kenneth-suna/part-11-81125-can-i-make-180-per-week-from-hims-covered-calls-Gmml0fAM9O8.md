## Summary
Kenneth demonstrates a real-money covered call selling strategy across three stocks (D-Wave, Archer Aviation, HIMS) targeting weekly premiums. He generated $633 in net premium income on a $14,000 deployed capital base (4.5% weekly return) while managing concurrent cash-secured put obligations and maintaining vacation flexibility.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Sell covered calls expiring Friday for weekly premium collection
2. Target 4-5% weekly premium yields on covered call positions
3. Deploy $5,000 per position for consistent weekly income generation
4. Maintain cash reserves equal to potential assignment obligations (e.g., $15,000 for three CSP contracts at $5,000 each)
5. Execution time: ~20 minutes research + 30 minutes trading work per week
6. Premium percentages achieved: D-Wave 4.6%, Archer Aviation 5.3%, HIMS 4%
7. Role premium as income (tax view) even if rolling reduces net profit temporarily
