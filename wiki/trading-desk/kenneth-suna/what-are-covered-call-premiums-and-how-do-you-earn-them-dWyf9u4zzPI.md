## Summary
Kenneth Suna teaches the mechanics of covered call selling for weekly income. Buy 100 shares, immediately sell a covered call at $1-1.50 out-of-the-money strike expiring Friday, and earn premium income plus capital gains if assigned. He reports making ~$2,000/week using this method and emphasizes the dual profit mechanism: premium retention regardless of outcome, plus $1/share profit if shares are called away.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Minimum 100 shares required per covered call contract
2. Sell strike price $1-$1.50 out-of-the-money from purchase price (e.g., buy at $55, sell $56 call)
3. Use weekly Friday expiration (buy Monday, expire Friday)
4. Earn premium regardless of assignment outcome
5. If assigned, earn additional $1/share profit (e.g., $100 on 100 shares bought at $55, assigned at $56)
6. If stock doesn't breach strike, keep shares + premium and sell new call next week
7. Example: HIMS at $42/share, sell $43 call, earn $175 premium on $4,279 position
