## Summary
Kenneth demonstrates making $1,200 in premium while on vacation by selling covered calls on existing portfolio positions (HIMS, Nphase, QuantumCape). He emphasizes the importance of not chasing maximum gains but consistently capturing premium, even when opportunities arise mid-vacation.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Sell covered calls on high-conviction positions already held in portfolio to generate passive premium
2. When stock price climbs significantly, reassess covered call opportunities (QuantumCape jumped mid-week, enabling new $166 premium sale)
3. Accept assignment as natural outcome of covered call strategy rather than regret missing further upside
4. Target consistent 2-3% returns on covered call positions rather than maximizing gains
5. Can execute trades opportunistically with minimal time commitment (checked market during museum break)
6. Accumulate positions early in week if premiums are lucrative, before taking vacation
7. Portfolio goal: generate premium income consistently, not necessarily capture all possible stock appreciation
