## Summary
Kenneth demonstrates his weekly covered call strategy on Hims, selling 1-week-out calls $1 above his $56 purchase price ($57 strike) to generate ~$180-205 per week in premium. He discusses the trade-off between higher strike prices (58-59) that risk early assignment versus rolling contracts to retain shares when stock moves significantly.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Sell covered calls 1 week out ($1 above purchase price for predictable entry)
2. Target ~$180-205 premium per covered call on 100-share positions
3. Monitor for early assignment risk when underlying moves significantly ITM before Friday expiration
4. Consider rolling contracts to following week to preserve shares rather than repurchasing
5. Track weekly premium accumulation systematically in a playlist series
6. Current position: 100 shares Hims bought at $56, high probability of $58 strike assignment by June 20th expiration
7. Monitor geopolitical/macro events (Israel-Iran tensions mentioned) that can cause rapid directional moves mid-week
