## Summary
Kenneth demonstrates a weekly covered call selling strategy targeting $180/week premium. He reviews 5 positions (HIMS, Joby, NPHASE, OPEN, QBTS), discusses timing mistakes made by selling calls too early in a down week, and demonstrates rolling expired calls out one week to capture higher premium as stocks recovered.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Sell covered calls with 1-week expiration for maximum premium capture
2. Roll calls out 1 week when underlying stock price rises above strike price (keeps same strike for highest premium)
3. Avoid selling premium too early in volatile weeks; wait for recovery to sell at higher strikes/prices
4. Track cost basis and premium per position: NPHASE $7,500 cost for $110 premium (1.5%), OPEN $5,000 cost for $146 premium (2.9%)
5. Rolling mechanics: Buy back expired call at loss, immediately sell new 1-week call at same strike price to net additional premium
6. HIMS position: sold $49 strike call for $70 premium when stock closed at $48 (near ATM)
7. Weekly portfolio target: $180-$950+ range depending on rolling activity and stock recovery timing
