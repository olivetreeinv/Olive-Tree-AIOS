## Summary
Kenneth Suna demonstrates his covered call strategy generating approximately $1,100/week in premium income from his portfolio, illustrating real positions with both winners and losers while emphasizing the flexibility of capital management when shares get called away.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Generate ~$1,100/week selling covered calls on invested capital
2. When shares get called away, freed-up capital can be redeployed to new positions
3. Monitor weekly decision points: decide whether to hold losing positions or sell for losses
4. Covered calls can be applied across multiple holdings simultaneously (demonstrated with QuantumScape, Nphase, HIMS, Oscar Health positions)
5. Each weekly cycle presents opportunity to reassess positions and make tactical adjustments
