## Summary
Kenneth Suna demonstrates his covered call income strategy targeting 2-4% weekly returns on volatile stocks. He explains the calculation method (premium divided by cost basis, multiplied by 100) and emphasizes consistency over maximum gains, showing real examples like Olo ($241 premium on $5,685 cost = 4.23% return).

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Calculate covered call return percentage: (premium ÷ cost basis) × 100
2. Target 2-4% weekly return per covered call position
3. Sell covered calls on volatile stocks to generate weekly income
4. Accept assignment willingly - focus on premium, not share ownership
5. Do not chase 'what could have been' - avoid comparing to alternative stock trading outcomes
6. Use covered calls as retirement income stream even after stopping active trading
7. Volatility increases premium prices, making compensation better in volatile markets
