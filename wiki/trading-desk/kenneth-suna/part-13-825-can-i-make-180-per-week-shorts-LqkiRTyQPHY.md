## Summary
Kenneth Suna demonstrates his weekly covered call strategy generating ~$1,000/week on a $32,853 portfolio (3% weekly return). He shares specific positions, premium collected, and key lessons learned about position sizing and avoiding over-concentration in high-risk stocks.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target ~3% weekly premium return on covered call positions
2. Avoid large position sizes (>$5,000) in high-risk/volatile stocks like QuantumCape
3. Reduced position size from $5,000 to $2,500 in risky names after learning from mistakes
4. Execute buy and sell covered calls on the same day (Monday) when possible
5. Accept <1% premium on positions near purchase price to maintain portfolio activity
6. Monitor stocks approaching purchase price to identify covered call opportunities
7. Nphase position size ~$7,600 generated 3.2% premium in one week
