## What it does
Weekly content engine. Two modes: **Ideation** (scans live sources, generates 5–7 ranked blog ideas as a menu) and **Draft** (writes a full blog post in Olive Tree voice from the chosen idea).

## Trigger phrases
- `/marketing`
- "blog ideas"
- "what should I write about this week"
- "generate blog ideas"
- "write a blog"
- "newsletter ideas"

## What it reads
| File | Why |
|---|---|
| `references/news-research.md` | Source stack, rate tables, blog angle pairings |
| `references/voice.md` | Brian's voice — direct, numbers-first, no filler |
| `references/buy-box.md` | Active markets — stay consistent with geographies |
| `context/about-me.md` | Brian's background and mission |
| `context/about-business.md` | Olive Tree's structure and investor types |

## Live sources scanned (WebSearch)
- CRE Daily (`site:credaily.com multifamily`, last 7 days)
- GlobeSt (`site:globest.com multifamily`, last 7 days)
- Additional sources per `references/news-research.md`

## Output
**Ideation mode:** 5–7 ranked blog ideas with angle + hook. Brian picks one.
**Draft mode:** Full blog post in Olive Tree voice, ready to publish or edit.

## Notes
- Always read all reference files before generating ideas or drafts.
- Blog angles must tie to Brian's active markets and investor audience.
