## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches how to build trust and relationships with commercial real estate brokers to access deal flow in the 15-50 unit multifamily space. The key is demonstrating you can close deals through resume credibility, learning terminology, touring properties strategically, and networking at conferences—all foundational activities before brokers will seriously consider you for off-market opportunities.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Resume is non-negotiable for 20+ unit deals; if you don't have one, partner with someone who does
2. You must prove to brokers you can 'raise the equity, pull down the debt, close the escrow'
3. Tour deals for 5-6 months in advance even with 'no intent' to execute, just to build relationships
4. Learn commercial terminology so brokers know 'you understand how the game's played'
5. Submit letters of intent on deals you don't plan to close to 'show your movement in the marketplace'
6. Attend multifamily conferences across different regions to network and get broker referrals (e.g., 'I know John in San Antonio, I'll connect you')
7. Brokers ultimately want three things: proof you can close, that you won't embarrass them, and to know who you are
