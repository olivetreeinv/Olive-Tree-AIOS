## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches preliminary questions to ask brokers and during property tours to evaluate markets and deals. The focus is on understanding market fundamentals (employers, jobs, cap rates, price per unit) and deal-specific terms (whisper price, deposit amounts, closing timeline) to properly underwrite value-add multifamily deals and build broker relationships.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Ask about main employers and job diversity—'jobs, jobs, jobs' drive population and tenant base
2. Understand going-in cap rates (current acquisition pricing) vs. terminal/exit cap rates (post-renovation valuation)
3. Get price per door metrics: unrenovated as-is price, and renovated price per door for comparable class properties (A/B/C)
4. Request whisper price from broker—what the seller is hoping to get, typically trending over whisper price 9 times out of 10 in recent markets
5. Ask about specific terms: deposit amount, hard money amount required day one, closing period, due diligence period
6. Identify development paths and city planning to understand where redevelopment is happening and employers will follow
7. Build relationships with brokers to get market intel on competitive offer trends, deposit amounts, and hard money ranges
