## Summary
Justin Brennan walks through the psychology and systematic process of investing out-of-state, emphasizing the need to move beyond high-cost coastal markets to scale wealth. He details how to properly set up logistics, operations, and market knowledge 6-8 months in advance before executing any deals, including property management, construction teams, and legal/accounting infrastructure.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Invest in purple or red states only for fairness to landlords and tenants
2. Set up logistics, operations, property management, construction teams, and legal/accounting 6-8 months in advance before striking a deal
3. Drive the target market extensively to understand neighborhoods, path of progress, major employers, schools, universities, and city infrastructure investments
4. Install 6-8 cameras on every property to monitor in real-time from anywhere in the country
5. Establish relationships with multiple lenders, brokers, property managers, and construction teams before deals come across your desk
6. When deals start coming, you should already know the neighborhood intimately - street quality, nearby retail, schools, upcoming developments
7. Fail fast with underperforming team members (property managers, construction teams) and replace them quickly rather than delay
