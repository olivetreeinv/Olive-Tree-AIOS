## Summary
Justin teaches a step-by-step process for identifying and building relationships with 3-4 brokers in a chosen market to gain access to off-market deal flow. By touring on-market deals, exchanging business cards face-to-face, and positioning yourself as a serious buyer, you request placement on brokers' off-market/pre-market lists to access deals before public listing.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target 'mom and pop' brokers and smaller complexes under 8-10, 20, 30-unit deals to get direct access to decision-makers like the listing broker, not assistants
2. Attend property tours in person ('belly to belly, eyeball to eyeball') and exchange business cards to build rapport
3. After touring, call the broker to follow up, mention your underwritten price (even if significantly lower), acknowledge the gap without criticizing, then ask: 'Do you have any other deals like this that maybe are on market, that are not on market yet?'
4. Request to be put on broker's email list specifically for 'off-market first look deals' and pre-market opportunities
5. Build a network of 3-5 brokers in your chosen market to create consistent deal flow from pre-market/off-market sources
6. Start with on-market deals as leverage to gain credibility and move to off-market deals ('on to off, on to off, step by step')
7. Establish professional presence with logo, business card, and website so you 'act the part, look the part' as a legitimate real estate investor
