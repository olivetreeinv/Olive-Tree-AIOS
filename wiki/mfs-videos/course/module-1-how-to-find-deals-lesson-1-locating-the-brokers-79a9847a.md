## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches how to locate commercial brokers and deals in the 5+ unit multifamily space, emphasizing relationship-building over portals like LoopNet. He stresses starting small (5-10 units) with local brokers before scaling to larger deals, requiring either capital, operational experience, or partnership with experienced syndicators.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Avoid LoopNet—deals listed there are 'way overpriced'; go direct to brokerage websites and their email lists instead
2. For 5+ unit commercial space, work with smaller local/regional brokers initially, not mega firms like CBRE/JLL/Marcus & Millichap
3. Brokers won't give time to first-time syndicators—you must go through local agents, not top brokers
4. Start with 2-4 units, graduate to 5-10 units with local brokers, then move to 25+ units once you have capital/experience
5. At 25+ units you're dealing with 'bigger zeros'—non-refundable deposits in hundreds of thousands and massive legal fees; one mistake ends your career
6. Build broker relationships through conferences, websites, email campaigns, phone calls, and 'boots on the ground' networking over time
7. You must bring value to partnerships: either capital, sweat equity, or operational experience—otherwise partners have no reason to work with you
