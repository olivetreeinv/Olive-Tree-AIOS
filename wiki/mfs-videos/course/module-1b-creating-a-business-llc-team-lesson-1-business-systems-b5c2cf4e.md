## Summary
Justin Brennan covers foundational business systems for launching a multifamily operating company, including entity formation, accounting software, CRM/investor portals, websites, and lead generation funnels. While not directly addressing Brian's deal sourcing or underwriting drag, these systems are prerequisites for scaling a multifamily business.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Form your LLC in the state where you live, not where properties are located, to avoid tax issues and establish your holdings company
2. You can form an LLC directly via Secretary of State website for $100-200 instead of paying LegalZoom $1000+
3. Use QuickBooks for accounting software and a CRM+portal system like SyndicationPro ($100s vs thousands) to manage investors and leads
4. Build website on Wix.com or hire freelancers on Fiverr.com for $100-300 to create professional frontage
5. Create lead funnels and lead magnets (content, meetup groups, educational resources) to attract investors and partners into your ecosystem
6. When bringing in other investors, create a single-purpose LLC for each asset in the state where the property is located
7. You need both analytical/operational and visionary personality types on your team—hire or partner with the type you lack
