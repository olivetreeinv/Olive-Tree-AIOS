## Summary
Justin teaches how to use Crexie.com to identify brokers and properties in target markets, then leverage on-market listings to build broker relationships and gain access to pre-market deals. The key strategy is contacting brokers about on-market properties that didn't sell pre-market, touring them to build rapport, and using that relationship to access the real deal flow.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. On-market deals (Crexie, LoopNet, CoStar) represent only 40% or less of available inventory; best deals sell pre-market to broker databases 2-3 weeks before listing sites
2. On-market deals that appear online 'didn't sell pre-market, so therefore the broker said okay, let's take and see what kind of chump we can get on the real market'
3. Filter Crexie by unit size and cap rate to narrow results in your target market
4. Click the OM (Offering Memo) button to access broker's marketing package with property details, photos, financials, unit mix, and pro forma
5. Call the broker: ask if property was in escrow, current offers, active offers, and tour availability; frame it as 'I'm super interested in this market in particular'
6. The entire purpose is building rapport: 'you are not going to get access to the real deals that make sense if you do not build rapport'
7. 'Leverage the on to get to the off' - execute this with 3 properties in same market/submarket; expect most won't work but they give access to pre-market inventory where deals make financial sense
