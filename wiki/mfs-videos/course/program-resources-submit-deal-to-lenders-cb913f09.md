## Summary
Justin demonstrates how to submit deals to lender partners through the AIOS portal backend system. He reviews three lending partners (Walker Dunlap, MVP Capital, Groves Capital) and their specializations—Freddie/Fannie Mae direct conduit lending, CMBS/renovation loans, and creative nationwide capital sourcing respectively. This resource streamlines lender access but doesn't directly address Brian's stated pain points of deal sourcing or underwriting.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Walker Dunlap specializes in Freddie/Fannie Mae direct conduit with typical $1 million+ loan amounts and non-recourse debt terms
2. Walker Dunlap can broker CMBS loans with local commercial banks and provide renovation components alongside acquisition loans
3. MVP Capital targets CMBS and local commercial bank loans specifically for value-add deals with medium-to-heavy construction components
4. Groves Capital operates nationwide and handles 'any types of capital, fix and flips, apartment deals, any types of commercial real estate, any types of loans' with creative structuring
5. All three lenders operate nationwide and can handle 99.999% of commercial real estate loan types
6. Portal submission process includes direct application forms (e.g., Groves Capital link) plus direct lender contact information for email outreach
