## Summary
Justin Brennan discusses multiple ways to invest in real estate, using his first 4-unit deal in San Diego ($425K purchase, $1.1M sale after 6 years) as an example of the DIY approach where he controlled everything as broker, property manager, and operator. He emphasizes that while full control is a benefit, self-funding has limits and recommends combining DIY deals with syndication and partnerships to scale.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Buy your first deals yourself to maintain 100% control
2. Start with your own investment capital plus debt financing
3. Combine DIY deals with syndication/partnerships to scale beyond personal capital limits
4. Use early DIY wins (e.g., $425K→$1.1M in 6 years) to fund out-of-state syndication
5. Don't rely solely on full control—diversify through partnerships and crowdfunding sources
