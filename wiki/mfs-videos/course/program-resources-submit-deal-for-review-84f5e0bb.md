## Summary
Justin Brennan walks through the operational process for submitting deals for underwriting review through the AIOS portal. He specifies the four required documents (OM, rent roll, T12 P&L, and completed deal analyzer) and the submission email address to facilitate quick feedback on deal analysis.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Submit four pieces: OM (property marketing flyer), rent roll, T12 (12-month P&L statement), and completed underwriting in deal analyzer
2. Email deals to support@multifamilyschool.com with property address and 'deal for review' in subject line
3. Submit underwriting in Excel format, ideally all documents via email
4. Same submission process applies to LOI (letter of intent) offers on properties
5. Use the portal's deal analyzer or your own preferred underwriting tool, submit in Excel
