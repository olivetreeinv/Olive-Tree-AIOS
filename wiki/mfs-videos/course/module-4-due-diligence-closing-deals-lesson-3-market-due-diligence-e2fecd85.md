## Summary
Justin teaches systematic market due diligence by personally visiting comps, researching major employers, tracking population growth trends (targeting 1%+ annually), and studying municipal development websites for future infrastructure projects. He emphasizes that boots-on-the-ground research often uncovers pricing discrepancies with broker OMs and reveals competitive advantages investors will respect.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Visit and walk all comparable properties in person—never rely solely on online maps or apartments.com
2. Broker OMs can be 3+ months outdated; your on-the-ground comp research generates insider information that counters stale data
3. Target markets with population growth of 1% per year or more
4. Research major employers and understand where tenant base works and lives
5. Check city development websites for approved/pending projects: light rail, industrial, retail, airports, parks
6. Identify 'path of progress' infrastructure like light rail systems coming within 2-3 years—these drive people, jobs, and rents
7. Know your comps in detail so you can defend your rent projections with data and facts against lender/investor pushback
