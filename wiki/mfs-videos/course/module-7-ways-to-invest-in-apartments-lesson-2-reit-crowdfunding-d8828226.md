## Summary
Justin explains REITs and crowdfunding platforms (CrowdStreet, Fundrise) as alternative real estate investment vehicles that provide liquidity and access to institutional-quality assets without direct property management. While useful for portfolio diversification, these are passive investments that don't directly address Brian's core pain points of deal sourcing and underwriting for value-add multifamily acquisitions.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. 90% of all profits from a REIT must be paid out to investors
2. CrowdStreet and Fundrise democratize access to institutional quality assets that are highly vetted
3. REITs offer good liquidity and can be traded like stocks
4. Private REITs provide less liquidity but more control than public REITs
5. Justin recommends diversifying across doing deals yourself, REITs, and syndications/partnerships
