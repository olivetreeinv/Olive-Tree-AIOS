## Summary
Justin Brennan provides a brief tutorial on how to book weekly accountability calls with a success coach through the AIOS platform backend. The video is administrative guidance about accessing mentorship support resources rather than deal-specific content.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Book a call with your success coach once per week maximum
2. Access the booking link by scrolling to 'book a call with your coach' in the backend
3. Calls are in addition to weekly sessions with Justin and the team
