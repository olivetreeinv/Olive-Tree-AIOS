## Key Takeaways
1. Keep all-in business expenses at 19% of gross income to preserve capital for investing
2. Realtor-investors need two commission jobs: selling homes (9-5) + deal sourcing/underwriting (7pm-midnight) to transition from agent to principal
3. Real estate will be tokenized on blockchain via platforms like Red Swan and Own Prop, allowing fractional ownership and liquidity without refinancing
4. Start with 2-4 unit rental properties to learn underwriting before advancing to sophisticated deals
5. Maintain 6 months cash reserves in fiat; invest remainder in tangible assets (real estate, metals, crypto) ahead of USD currency shift (2025-2028 timeline)
6. Morning routine: gratitude practice, 20 minutes no phone, 20-min peloton + 10-min meditation—critical for energy to execute both agent and investor roles
7. Success formula: Goodwill (free content) + Freebie Offer × Frequency = Revenue; apply this to attract deal partners and capital sources
