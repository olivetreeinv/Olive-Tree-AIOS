## Summary
Justin Brennan's mentorship video titled 'Why Your Wishes Aren't Coming True' focuses on manifestation philosophy and personal development rather than multifamily deal analysis. The content centers on recognizing opportunities, taking action on subtle universe deliveries, and mindset shifts around fear, faith, and purpose rather than deal-specific underwriting or sourcing tactics.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Step one: ask the universe; step two: pay attention when universe starts to deliver it
2. Recognize subtle signals like a book or a person walking into a room that represents opportunity
3. When you see the turn/lane opening, you must turn immediately or you'll be 'miles and miles behind'
4. Manifestation requires 'relentless execution'—consistency matters more than timing
5. Procrastination is opportunity's 'natural assassin'
6. Most people fail at manifestation because they don't take action out of fear and scarcity mindset
7. The difference between chasing purpose and living purpose: chasing is an imaginary dangling thing; living purpose means life shines in front of you and you're present to it
