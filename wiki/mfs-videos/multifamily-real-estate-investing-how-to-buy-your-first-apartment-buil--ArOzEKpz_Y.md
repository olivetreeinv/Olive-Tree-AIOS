## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches the complete process for buying 15-50 unit multifamily properties, including deal sourcing through brokers, underwriting using NOI-based metrics (14-20% annualized returns, 6-10% cash-on-cash, 1.8x+ equity multiple), and structuring deals with 10% GP capital to control $1M+ assets. He demonstrates how to raise equity from investors by presenting deals with strong financial metrics and positioning as General Partner to capture multiple revenue streams (acquisition fees, asset management, cash flow splits, depreciation benefits).

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target 10-30 unit deals for first investments to get broker callbacks and manage capital raises efficiently
2. Leverage broker relationships on-market to access 60%+ of off-market pre-market deals that never hit websites
3. Deal value formula: Building Value = NOI / Cap Rate; every $1,000/month NOI boost adds $220,000 in value
4. Metrics that attract investors: 14-20% annualized return, 6-10% cash-on-cash, 1.8x+ equity multiple minimum
5. Capital stack structure: 70% debt/30% equity typical; GP brings 10% cash but gets 30% of deal economics (double-dip)
6. Acquisition fees typically 2-3% of purchase price, asset management 1-2% of revenue, and 30-40% of cash flow/profits
7. Raising $300K-500K equity on first deals realistic via family/friends/investors when deal metrics justify 14-20%+ returns
