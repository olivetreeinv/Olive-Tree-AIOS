## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches a comprehensive framework for analyzing multifamily deals using five pillars: find, analyze, finance, close, and manage. He demonstrates real deal analyzers submitted by mentees, showing how to properly underwrite properties using realistic expense assumptions and key metrics (IRR, cash-on-cash return, equity multiple) rather than broker fluff. He emphasizes finding deals that pencil at 14-20% IRR, 6-10% cash-on-cash, and 1.8-2.0x equity multiple to attract investor capital.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Use 12-15% economic loss (vacancy + lost to lease + bad debt + concessions) for non-coastal markets, not the 4-5% brokers advertise
2. Value = NOI ÷ Cap Rate; with two variables you can solve for the third
3. Target deal metrics: IRR 14-20%, Cash-on-Cash 6-10%, Equity Multiple 1.8-2.0+
4. 60% of commercial deals trade in pre-market before hitting websites like LoopNet; leverage on-market deals to identify brokers and access their pre-market inventory
5. Tour 3 properties with 3 different brokers to break bread and build relationships for off-market deal flow
6. Structure: bring 10% equity as GP (general partner) to control deal; raise remaining 30% equity (total 40%) from limited partners; debt covers 60-70%
7. Acquisition fee: 2-3% of purchase price covers your GP compensation at close; allows controlling deals with minimal personal capital
