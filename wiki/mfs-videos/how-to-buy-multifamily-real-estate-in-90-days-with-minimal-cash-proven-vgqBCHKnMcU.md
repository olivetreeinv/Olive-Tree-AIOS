## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches a comprehensive framework for buying 15-50 unit multifamily properties in 90 days with minimal cash ($4,500-$42,000 out of pocket). He demonstrates how to structure deals using debt (65-70% LTV) and equity (30-35%), leverage broker relationships to access off-market deals, and use acquisition fees and asset management fees to reduce personal capital requirements while maintaining control as the general partner.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target deals with 14-20%+ annual cash-on-cash returns, 6-10%+ cash flow, and 1.8x+ equity multiples before proceeding
2. Increase NOI by $1,000/month = $220,000 in property value increase (NOI ÷ cap rate formula)
3. Only 40% of multifamily deals appear on websites like CoStar; 60% are pre-market—build broker relationships to access them
4. Structure equity raise with 10% GP investment to control 30% ownership while limited partners invest 90% for 70% ownership
5. Get commercial loans on property financials (not personal credit)—65-70% LTV on total project cost easier than residential mortgages
6. Earn 5-8 revenue streams per deal: 2-3% acquisition fee, 1-2% asset management fee, 30-40% cash flow, 30-40% profits, tax write-offs, construction management fees (5-8%), disposition fees, and finder fees
7. On a $1.25M purchase price deal: $100K closing costs + $170K renovations = $1.52M all-in; 70% loan = $1.06M; $420K equity needed; GP contributes $42K (10%), receives $37,500 acquisition fee = $4,500 net out-of-pocket
