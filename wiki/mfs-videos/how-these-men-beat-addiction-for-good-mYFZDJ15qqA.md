## Key Takeaways
1. Implement 12-week business planning cycles instead of 12-month plans to create urgency and accountability (March 31st deadlines instead of December 31st)
2. Pick 4-5 high-quality conferences per year and buy the best ticket available to access high-level networks, not for general information
3. Maintain consistent follow-up pressure on contractors/consultants through kind, gentle check-ins while maintaining project momentum, especially through holidays
4. Micromanage until you can trust—reliability is the determining factor in professional relationships and team promotion
5. Pain motivation typically outweighs pleasure motivation for actual behavior change and goal completion
6. Identify root causes through detailed life timeline exercises (blocks of time from childhood to present) to reprogram subconscious beliefs
7. Don't identify people by their problems—reframe identity positively (e.g., 'person with alcohol problem' not 'alcoholic')
