## Summary
Justin Brennan demonstrates a 5-step system for sourcing off-market multifamily deals through direct broker outreach, cold calling, and relationship building. The video focuses heavily on deal sourcing methodology via broker calls in multiple markets (Ohio, Oklahoma, Texas), with secondary content on capital raising foundations, tax strategy (bonus depreciation), and entity structuring. ## Key Takeaways
1. Set up 'business in a box' foundation (LLC, website, CRM) before deal sourcing to build credibility with brokers and investors; without this, brokers won't trust you with deal flow
2. Build a 'list of 25-30' soft-committed capital partners in your CRM/database before getting deals under contract—this allows you to raise 3/4 of capital within 2 weeks when deal is locked
3. Use 'who do you know' framing instead of 'asking for money'; shift mentality from asking to providing opportunity (15-20% annual returns + tax write-offs via bonus depreciation)
4. Cold call brokers with buy-box definition (10-50 units, $2-3M price point for GA/TN/AL) to establish relationships; ask for referrals on lenders, property managers, insurance—brokers are 'gatekeepers' to local ecosystem
5. On auctions with reserve prices, ask broker directly: 'What's your whisper price/guidance?' (e.g., $50-60k/door comps); financeable auctions allow non-cash offers if you move quickly
6. Market trip: spend 3 days on ground touring neighborhoods, knowing rents/school zones/bad areas, meeting 2-3 brokers + 2-3 property managers + 2-3 lenders—do this before deal flow starts
7. Bonus depreciation: with 100% depreciation available in 2025, ~$1 cost seg study (post-close) yields almost dollar-for-dollar K1 write-off in year 1; spreads over 5 years; recapture minimal if held 5 years
