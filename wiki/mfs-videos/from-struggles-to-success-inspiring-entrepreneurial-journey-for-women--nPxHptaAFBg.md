## Summary
This is a podcast interview between Justin Brennan (multifamily mentor) and Kristen Williams (event strategist) focused on Kristen's entrepreneurial journey, event production expertise, and coaching business models. While Justin mentions his multifamily schooling community vision, the conversation centers on Kristen's background in event strategy for Tony Robbins, Dean Graziosi, and Russell Brunson rather than deal-specific multifamily instruction.

## Key Takeaways
1. Event conversion framework: Structure content in specific order with offer at specific time to achieve 20% conversion on high-ticket offers ($110k+)
2. Virtual event show-up rate: 85% attendance for paid events with strong email sequences and swag
3. Three-day event structure: Deliver content themed around core promise (e.g., 'Get Your First Deal'), then make offer on day 2-3 with repitch strategy in subsequent sessions
4. Marketing timeline: Begin advertising 8 weeks before event; pull from existing email list and supplement with paid ads and free masterclass funnels
5. Tiered offer strategy: Create price anchors (e.g., $10k coaching vs $25k done-for-you) to capture both new and advanced buyers without confusion
6. Event logistics formula: Start with sales strategy and offer design first, then reverse-engineer content to taste the offer, then handle production/logistics/team training
7. Subscriber model risk: Subscriptions create high churn; structure as 12-month commitment with juicy deliverables at end to prevent cancellation
