## Key Takeaways
1. When your spouse shares emotions, ask 'Do you want my voice or my ears right now?' - your job is to hold space, not solve problems
2. Men need: supportive spouse, peace in home, physical intimacy. Women need: feeling provided for/protected, emotional safety, emotional intimacy
3. The 'shoreline' analogy: be stable like a shoreline while waves (her emotions) come and go at different heights
4. Four attachment styles determine relationship patterns: secure, anxious, avoidant, fearful-avoidant - mostly formed in childhood
5. Hypnotic reprogramming in meditative state can reframe traumatic memories and break limiting beliefs planted in subconscious
6. Never keep more than 6 months cash reserves in US bank accounts - diversify into tangible assets (real estate, crypto stablecoins, treasuries) to hedge currency devaluation
7. The $10,000 check accountability mechanism: pain/fear drives decisions more than pleasure - use external commitment to force execution
