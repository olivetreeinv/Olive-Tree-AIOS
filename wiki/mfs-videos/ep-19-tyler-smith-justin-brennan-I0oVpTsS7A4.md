## Key Takeaways
1. Time is finite—calculate remaining days (lifespan minus current age times 365) and write it on a whiteboard to combat procrastination
2. Don't give time to people who don't value it; relationships require mutual deposits (relationship capital/bank accounts), not one-sided withdrawals
3. Master your morning to master your day: wake early, meditate/read 10-15 minutes, block time the night before by hour to schedule accomplishments
4. Success attracts the people you want; struggle attracts the people you need—both matter but serve different purposes
5. Core purpose from the Seven Mountains (media, family, business, religion, sports, arts, government) is essential to avoid complacency after initial success
6. Faith and prayer create mental clarity and resilience during crisis; specific daily practice (6am prayer, praise, sermon) can accelerate healing and decision-making
7. Homelessness has multiple distinct causes (mental illness, addiction, voluntary detachment from society) requiring different solutions—one-size-fit-all approaches waste resources
