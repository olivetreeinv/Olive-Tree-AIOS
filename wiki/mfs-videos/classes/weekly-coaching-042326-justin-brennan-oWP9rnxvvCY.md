## Summary
Justin Brennan conducts live broker outreach calls across AR/TN/AL markets, demonstrating deal sourcing methodology and how to build relationships with brokers who control off-market inventory. He emphasizes using Crexy as a broker Rolodex tool, identifying market makers by logo frequency, and positioning oneself as a serious buyer through operational setup (CRM, lenders, mock deals).

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Identify market makers by scanning Crexy logos repeatedly—same brokers appearing multiple times indicate they control deal flow
2. Target 10-30 unit B/C class assets, ideally built before 2000s, priced $50k-$150k per unit to control capital (e.g., 20 units @ $100k/unit = $2M deal, $600k down payment, $60k out-of-pocket after acquisition fee)
3. Use the initial property call as a 'lure'—don't just pitch that deal; mention broader market interest ('we're looking at coming into Arkansas') to trigger broker to share off-market pipeline
4. Keep buy box high-level conversationally; don't send formal buy box immediately, build rapport first over time
5. Underwrite on fundamentals: 65-70% LTV, mid-teens IRR target, 8-15% economic loss depending on market (exceptions only in CA, S. Florida, Northeast at 5-6%)
6. Set up full operational infrastructure (website, CRM, lenders with soft quotes, mock deals, advisory board) so brokers see you as serious; this unlocks off-market deal access after in-person market trips
7. Follow up aggressively: send aggressive LOIs to create conversation hooks, then follow up with brokers to discuss numbers rather than waiting passively
