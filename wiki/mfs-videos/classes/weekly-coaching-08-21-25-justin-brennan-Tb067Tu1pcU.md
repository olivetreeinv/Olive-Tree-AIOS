## Summary
Justin Brennan delivers a coaching session emphasizing resilient mindset and consistent daily discipline as critical to deal sourcing success. While most of the session focuses on mindset philosophy, health optimization, and personal development, the latter portion includes a 14-unit Texas deal walkthrough with Gideon demonstrating underwriting mechanics, pricing strategy, and investor waterfall analysis.

## Key Takeaways
1. Compound effect: daily penny doubled for 31 days = $10.7M; doubled every other day = only ~$327K—consistency separates winners from losers
2. Closed mouths don't get fed; hunt for deals and broker relationships daily—don't wait for brokers to come to you
3. Ask brokers for 'whisper price' or 'price guidance' before submitting LOI to anchor negotiation and understand market expectations
4. Underwrite deals using broker's stated numbers first, then adjust down to reality; expect broker numbers to be 20-30% optimistic in current market
5. Never operate <40-45% expense ratio on value-add buildings; resist broker pressure to 'work the proforma' instead of reality
6. For unpriced deals: target price should be $1-1.2M on $1.5M whisper; come in low with room to negotiate upward
7. Debt coverage ratio minimum 1.2-1.25x; if current rents below market, can refinance without renovation; if below-market rents require heavy value-add, need bridge debt not traditional financing
8. Three-person daily habits to escape negative mindset: list 3 gratitudes, ground barefoot on soil for vitamin D, play 5-7 song positivity playlist
