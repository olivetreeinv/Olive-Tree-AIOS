## Summary
Justin Brennan coaches on mindset, capital raising fundamentals, and deal-sourcing processes for Brian's team. While not directly analyzing specific deals, the session covers critical operational foundations (LLC setup, website presence, CRM systems, investor list building) and mindset techniques (daily vision prayer, neuroplasticity, pain vs. pleasure motivation) that directly enable deal sourcing and underwriting capacity.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Write and read a daily vision prayer twice daily (morning/evening) in present tense, specifying: (1) exact monthly income target with date, (2) income sources (job, multifamily, other), (3) giving-back plan; brain doesn't distinguish fiction from truth, so frequencies reprogram subconscious
2. Build 25-person CRM contact list within 48 hours with names, phone, emails to start capital attraction pipeline—these become soft commitments for future deal offerings
3. Set up LLC, professional website with advisory team section (include Brennan Polley Group as strategic partner), and CRM system before raising capital—'look the part' to gain broker/lender/investor confidence
4. When negative thoughts spiral, use three remedies in sequence: (1) state 3 things grateful for, (2) go barefoot outside to ground body and absorb vitamin D, (3) play 3-6 pre-selected positivity songs that reliably shift energy
5. Broker fees negotiated into deal price, not added to buyer costs—typical listing broker commission 4-6%, so negotiate buyer broker down to 2-2.5% to be paid from listing side
6. First deal can accept investment minimums below $25K (15-20K acceptable) but set baseline at $25K for LP management simplicity; use 506(b) offering (allows non-accredited investors, cannot publicly market) until volume justifies 506(c)
7. Use pain/pleasure motivation strategically—post-date accountability checks ($5-10K) to broker/mentor if deadline (e.g., December 31 deal lock) missed; fear-based motivation drives some personalities harder than pleasure incentives
