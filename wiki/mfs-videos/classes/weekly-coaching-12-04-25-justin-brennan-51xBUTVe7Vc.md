## Key Takeaways
1. Structure deals with 60/40 split (60% to LPs, 40% to GP promote) or adjust to 70/30 or 80/20 on first deal to hit 14-15% investor IRR target
2. GP earns: preferred return portion (6-8% standard), promote split on cash flow, plus one-time fees (acquisition 1-2%, construction management 8%, asset management 1% monthly on revenue, capital raise 2%, refi fee 0.5%)
3. Example: $12.9M deal generated $193k acquisition fee (7 days post-close), $160k construction fee, $52k refi fee, $50k capital raise fee, plus monthly asset management of ~1% on $130k/month revenue
4. Minimum investor check size: $25k baseline (can flex to $15k for right person), but ensure it's not >50% of investor's disposable income to avoid constant calls
5. Set 90-day goals quarterly rather than 12-month annual plans (read '12-Week Year' by Moran/Lennington)
6. Build 'soft commitments' pre-deal by sending sample pitch decks to network, creating landing pages with summary videos (15-min Loom screen recordings), collect investor info via CRM
7. Don't wait until January—Q4 is setup time; apply 'Atomic Habits' (James Clear) to build consistent deal-hunting and underwriting routines
