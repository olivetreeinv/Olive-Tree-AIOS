## Summary
Justin Brennan coaches multifamily investors on market setup and broker relationship building. He explains how to use Crexy not to find deals (which he says are mostly overpriced at 80% of market), but to identify market-dominating brokers by their logo frequency. The core strategy involves calling brokers on listed deals as a pretext to build relationships and access their pre-market deal flow, where 80% of actual deals sell before public listing.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Use Crexy to find brokers by logo frequency, not deals—brokers with 3+ listings are market makers with 3-4 more deals behind the scenes
2. Brokers filter off-market opportunities using loan maturity dates; properties with loans maturing in next 12 months are targets for refinance or sale conversations
3. Set up your market geographically (e.g., Ohio), then plan 2-3 day in-person trip to tour deals and meet brokers, PMs, and local lenders
4. Call brokers on Crexy-listed deals with script: 'I'm buying in this market, interested in this property, can I get on your preferred/pre-market list?'
5. Negotiations use price anchoring—start high (e.g., $100K concession), expect rejection, land on target range ($30-40K) after multiple counteroffers and cooling-off periods
6. Brokers give lists (not recommendations) of PM companies and local lenders to build your local team without broker endorsement
7. Stage 1 deals sell 80% of the time in broker's pre-market database of 25-50 investors; Stage 2 (Crexy) gets the rejects that need heavy negotiation
