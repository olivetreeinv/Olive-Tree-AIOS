## Summary
Justin Brennan conducts a Q&A mentorship session covering market selection strategies, deal sourcing approaches, and mindset/visualization techniques. While not directly analyzing specific deals, the discussion emphasizes broker relationships, three-day market reconnaissance missions, using AI tools for market research, and avoiding common pitfalls like Section 8 properties for first-time investors.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Plan a '3-day reconnaissance mission' to target market: tour 3 properties, meet 2-3 brokers, interview 2-3 PM companies, and connect with 3 renovation crews (the '3-3-3' rule)
2. Use ChatGPT/AI to identify markets by prompting: 'population growth north of 1%, job growth of 1%, median household income $60,000+' then dive deeper with follow-up questions
3. Build a 'vision letter' (one page, three paragraphs: personal/professional/health-wellness) written in future tense as of Dec 31, 2025, and read it every single day
4. 'Inch wide and a mile deep' - narrow to one primary market after analyzing three, then commit fully with pre-positioned team before deals flow
5. Only pursue Section 8/PSH deals if in your 'backyard' (20-minute drive) due to resident screening risk; guaranteed income not worth property damage/destruction risk for first deals
6. Face-to-face/belly-to-belly broker meetings create off-market deal flow that phone/Zoom cannot replicate; this breaks through the '60-90 day resistance point'
7. When evaluating markets, use AI to research city development plans: 'Tell me where the city is putting money in development' for best investment location insights
