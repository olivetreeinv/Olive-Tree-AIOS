## Summary
Justin Brennan coaches Brian Norton's AIOS multifamily students on broker relationship-building and deal flow strategy. The session covers how to identify market-maker brokers by analyzing listing logos on Crexy, making cold calls, following up persistently, and conducting in-person market trips to warm relationships. While directly applicable to Brian's deal-sourcing challenge, the session also covers capital-raising foundations and mock deals.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Identify market-maker brokers by frequency of logos on Crexy—brokers with most listings have 3+ off-market deals behind every 1 public listing
2. Call brokers on specific Crexy listings as conversation lure, then pivot to asking 'Do you have anything else you're working on behind the scenes?'
3. Send LOI immediately, then call/text broker same day saying 'It's aggressive but I'd like to go over numbers to see where I may be wrong'
4. Follow up with brokers once per week; don't blast 100, cultivate 3-4 quality relationships in each market
5. Make market trip 1-2 weeks after initial broker call, text ahead: 'We connected earlier, coming to town, love to grab coffee and put face to name'
6. Pre-market deals are the real opportunity—Crexy/Loopnet deals 'suck' so broker relationships give you access to deals before they list publicly
7. Brokers are cold because they receive many wishy-washy calls; you must be the 'hunter' not 'gatherer'—'closed mouths don't get fed'
