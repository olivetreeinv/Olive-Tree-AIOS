## Key Takeaways
1. Use seller financing by offering slightly above market price to unlock zero-down deals with operational runway (paid $350k over 39 months at $3,500/month vs. $300k cash for property that became $2.25M value)
2. 'Portray weakness to gain strength' - Present humility and limited resources to increase counterparty's willingness to negotiate favorable terms
3. Best fight of your life is not to get more, but to keep what you got - Protection mode is critical in business cycles
4. Mastermind moderator quality matters more than attendees - value transfer and net worth growth per member annually indicates effectiveness
5. Build brand as talent magnet - long-form content signals values to attract aligned team members who won't burn out after 2-3 years
6. Develop people, not just tasks - 80% of entrepreneurs are solopreneurs; become entrepreneur by building delegatable team
7. When facing adversity, shift immediately from weakness/sorrow state to powerful action state - use music/barefoot grounding to reset energy
