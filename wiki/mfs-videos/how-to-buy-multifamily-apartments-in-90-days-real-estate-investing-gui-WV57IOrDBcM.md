## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches a comprehensive 90-day framework for buying multifamily apartments (15-50 units), focusing on three critical pillars: finding deals through broker relationships, analyzing deals using specific financial metrics (14-20% annual returns, 6-10% cash-on-cash, 1.8x equity multiple), and raising capital through syndication. The methodology emphasizes targeting pre-market deals, understanding net operating income (NOI), and structuring deals with general/limited partnerships to create multiple revenue streams.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target 10-30 unit deals for first properties; brokers will respond and lenders will finance easier than 50+ unit deals
2. For every $1,000 monthly NOI increase, add $220,000 in property value
3. Aim for 14-20% annual returns, 6-10% cash-on-cash return, and 1.8x equity multiple minimum on deals
4. Contact 'mom and pop' brokers directly: 'Hey Austin, I'm looking at your 23-unit on Melrose Court, been on market 51 days, how's activity?'
5. 60% of deals sell pre-market before hitting websites; only overpriced deals appear online
6. General partner structure: bring 10% of money, get 30% of deal (acquisition fee 2-3%, asset management fee 1-2%, cash flow/profit splits, disposition fee)
7. Use fixed-rate debt (3-5-7-10 year terms, amortized over 30 years); never over-leverage; prioritize properties purchased 2021-2022 as distressed opportunities
