## Summary
Justin Brennan teaches how to acquire 10-30 unit multifamily deals in 90 days with only $20,000 out of pocket by leveraging Other People's Money (OPM), eliminating three limiting beliefs: that you need significant capital, that multifamily is too complex, and that deals are hard to find. He demonstrates the deal-sourcing strategy using direct-to-broker and direct-to-owner approaches, with specific emphasis on accessing off-market deals through broker relationships.

---

## Key Takeaways
1. Target 10-30 unit deals for first investment (not 50+ units) because brokers and lenders expect newer investors at this size
2. Use 70% loan-to-cost financing on multifamily deals since banks finance the asset/business, not the person (need only 600+ credit score)
3. Only 30% of deals appear on Crexi/LoopNet; 70% sell in pre-market to 25-50 broker contacts—use public listings as 'lures' to meet brokers and access off-market deals
4. Raise capital in $25,000 minimum increments from 5-7 investors for 10-30 unit deals expecting 15-20% annual returns
5. Receive acquisition fee (2-3% of closing costs) paid within 7 days of closing—on $1.65M deal = ~$30,000 returned, reducing out-of-pocket to ~$20K
6. Get paid 5-8 ways in multifamily: cash flow, profits, acquisition fee, asset management fee (1% monthly revenue), construction management fee, selling fees, finder's fees
7. 3-4 deals of 10-30 units typically generate $10,000-$20,000 monthly passive cash flow to leave traditional employment
