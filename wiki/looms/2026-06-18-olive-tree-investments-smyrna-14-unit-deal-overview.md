## Summary
This Loom presents an investment opportunity for a 14-unit apartment building in Smyrna, Georgia. Brian Norton of Olive Tree Investments outlines a six-year plan with a possible refinance at year four, targeting a 2X return with a 16 to 18 percent investor IRR, 143 percent total ROI, and a 6 percent preferred return. The proposed structure is a 70 to 30 split, with investors receiving 70 percent of cash flow including capital events, and the minimum investment is $25,000. He also describes renovation plans to raise rents from about $1,100 per unit average to $13 plus, and cites tax benefits and inflation hedging, with investor cash flow projections summarized as 6 percent average cash flow and cash return, and an equity multiple of 1.53 to 2.
