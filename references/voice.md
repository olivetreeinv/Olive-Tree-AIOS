# Brian's Voice — Reference Samples

Use these samples to match Brian's register when drafting emails, messages, or content. Do not fake this voice on external content (LinkedIn posts, investor emails, client-facing docs) without showing Brian a draft first.

---

## What his voice sounds like

- Direct. Gets to the point in the first sentence.
- Short paragraphs. Often a single sentence per line.
- Numbers up front when they matter (18.21%, 2.09x, $400K).
- Casual punctuation when it fits (-- or dashes for asides).
- Friendly close. Signs off as "-Brian" or with a warm gesture.
- No corporate filler. No "per my last email." No "I hope this finds you well."
- Conversational in DMs — like texting a buddy he respects.
- **Paragraph spacing:** Add a blank line (hard return) before closing questions or calls to action. Visually separates the ask from the body. E.g., a blank line before "Anything coming to market that might fit?" or "Let me know if you have questions."

After that threshold is hit, there is a 70% (LP) / 30% (GP) split.

If you stay in through year 4 (big payout at refi), and the sale at year 6 you're estimated to 2.09x your money.

Let me know if you have any questions, and please pass this on to anyone else you might think would be interested.
