# Olive Tree — Land Wholesaling Playbook

The full process, distilled from the Carson & Jackson business plan (Koerner Office) and adapted to Olive Tree's GA launch market (Bartow / Cartersville). Read `references/land-wholesale-buy-box.md` first — it's authoritative on markets and filters.

> Not legal advice. Contracts require a GA real-estate attorney review before use. Verify GA wholesaling/assignment legality + seller-disclosure practice before going live.

---

## The builder-first method

Standard wholesaling finds a deal then hunts a buyer. We invert it:
1. **Buyer first** — call a builder/developer; get their **buy box** (price/lot or price/acre, sizes, zips, conditions, close timeline).
2. **Shop below it** — find vacant lots from out-of-state owners and offer 10–20% under the builder's price.
3. **Lock + assign** — assignable contract with the seller; assign to the builder; collect the spread at the title company.

Why it wins: you know the deal has a buyer before you contact a single seller. One email and it's done.

**Transparency pitch to sellers:** tell them you work with builders, you'll pass it on in 4–6 weeks, and you make 8–10% in between. You guarantee their price, no realtor fees, no closing costs, ~3-week close. Often nets the seller as much as a 6-month MLS listing minus commissions.

## Seller profile (who to target)

Best seller = **out-of-state owner, bought 5+ years ago, never built, paying rising property tax.** Not actively selling, quietly motivated. In Bartow this is TX/MD/MI/FL owners sitting on 1–10 ac vacant tracts.

| Seller type | Motivation | Best channel |
|---|---|---|
| Out-of-state, long-held vacant | High (taxes, liquidity) | Mail (free at scale); call if phone available |
| Inherited land, no use | Med-high | Mail + personalized call |
| Owns multiple lots (package) | High — one yes = several deals | Same; flag same-owner multi-lots |
| Expired/stale listing | Very high | Call immediately |
| Adjacent neighbor | Variable; often pays above market | Call before closing with a builder |

## Outreach channels

| Channel | Cost | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Direct mail** (`/land-mail`) | ~$0.50–1/piece | **Free auto-scale** — county gives mailing addresses. Intro letter + pre-filled offer. Some sellers just sign and return. |
| **Cold call** (`/land-call`) | $0 + skip-trace | Needs a phone number first (manual True People Search free, or paid API). Best for learning the market. Brian dials first. |
| **Neighbor call** | $0 | Always offer the adjacent owner first look before the builder — they pay above market. |

Brian's choice for launch: **cold calls first** (to learn the patterns), with mail as the free scale layer.

## Deal-killers (what `/land-deal` checks)

Land has far fewer than houses; most are checkable online, and the feasibility clause means a kill = a termination email, no loss.

| Issue | How to spot | Impact |
|---|---|---|
| Wetlands | Aerial: gray/dead trees = standing water | Severe; can gut value |
| Protected wildlife | Call the city; ask what's protected at the address | Can block building |
| Slope | County GIS elevation; ask builder's tolerance | Big in hilly NW GA |
| No utilities / well-septic | County records; builder preference | Lowers value |
| Flood zone | FEMA / county GIS | Lowers demand |
| Main-road frontage | Any map | Builders/buyers avoid |
| Positive: seawall / corner | Aerial / records | Adjust offer up |

Ask each builder up front: *what kills a deal for you in this area?* Learn per-market.

## Contracts (see templates)

- **Assignability clause** — lets you assign to the builder without buying it yourself.
- **Feasibility contingency to closing** — exit any time, any reason, no penalty.
- **EMD timing** — $250 (or seller's ask) due at closing, not signing; the builder's assignment EMD funds it.
- Files: `templates/land-psa-template.md` (seller), `templates/land-assignment-template.md` (builder). **Attorney review required.**

## Economics

- Avg profit/deal ~$8,600; gross margin 90–95%; revenue is lumpy — judge quarterly, not monthly.
- High margin = high taxable income with few write-offs; work with a tax pro.

---

## Post-close (compounding)

- **$1,000 referral** — handwritten thank-you after every close; pay $1,000 cash for a referred deal that closes (it comes out of deal proceeds; ~12% CAC on an $8.6K deal).
- **Neighbor first-look** — call adjacent owners before/at listing to a builder.
- **Same-owner check** — they often own more lots; `/land-sellers` flags multi-lot owners.
- **Save every number** — "save me as [name] Land Guy"; many deals come months after the first no.
