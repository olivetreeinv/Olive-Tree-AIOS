## Project Catalog Gigs

### Gig 1 — Underwriting
> **I will underwrite your multifamily or commercial real estate deal**
>
> Get an institutional-grade acquisition model from an active multifamily Principal.
> You send the OM, T-12, and rent roll — I deliver a full pro forma with debt sizing,
> returns, and a one-page verdict on whether the deal works.
>
> **Three tiers:**
> - **Quick Screen ($250)** — Base-case model, 1 asset, key returns, go/no-go read. 2-day.
> - **Full Underwrite ($600)** — Custom model, value-add scenarios, stress cases,
>   sensitivity tables, summary memo. 4-day.
> - **Institutional ($1,500)** — Everything above plus JV waterfall, promote structure,
>   investor-ready deck. 7-day.
>
> All tiers include one revision round. Message me your deal before ordering and I'll
> confirm the right tier.

### Gig 2 — BPO
> **I will create a broker price opinion and property valuation**
>
> A defensible desktop valuation from someone who buys real estate, not just reports
> on it. Comps, condition adjustments, ARV, and a clear value range you can take to a
> lender, partner, or seller.
>
> **Two tiers:**
> - **Desktop BPO ($75)** — SFR or small multifamily, 3–5 comps, value range. 2-day.
> - **BPO + Strategy ($150)** — Full valuation grid, repair estimate, rent-vs-flip
>   recommendation. 3-day.

### Gig 3 — Market Analysis
> **I will analyze a real estate market for your investment decision**
>
> Know whether a market is worth your capital before you commit. I score submarkets on
> the seven criteria that actually move underwriting — rent trends, absorption, job and
> population growth, supply pipeline, and more.
>
> **Two tiers:**
> - **Market Snapshot ($150)** — One submarket, five key metrics, one-page scorecard
>   with verdict. 2-day.
> - **Market Deep Dive ($400)** — Full scorecard, rent and sales comps, supply pipeline,
>   charts, written go/no-go recommendation. 4-day.
