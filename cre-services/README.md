# CRE Services — Freelance Underwriting & Analysis

A productized service line: selling Brian's underwriting, BPO, and market-analysis
skills as fixed-price deliverables. First channel is **Upwork**; platform-agnostic
by design (could expand to direct referrals, Fiverr, or a landing page later).

**The edge:** Brian is an active multifamily Principal who underwrites with his own
investors' capital — not a junior Excel freelancer. The AIOS already produces ~80% of
each deliverable (`/market-research`, `/underwriting`, `/deal-analysis`), so margin is
high and turnaround is fast.
