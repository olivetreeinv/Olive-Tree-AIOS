# Service Offerings & Rate Plans

Pricing calibrated to 2025–2026 Upwork CRE rates (Perplexity sonar-pro, 2026-06-14).
Brian sits in the mid-to-senior band ($75–150/hr) on skill, but Upwork's algorithm
punishes new sellers with zero reviews — so **launch low, climb after ~5 reviews.**

Fixed-price productized offerings convert better than hourly on Upwork.

## 1. Multifamily Underwriting Model
Full acquisition pro forma — T-12/T-3 scrub, rent roll absorption, debt sizing,
returns (IRR, CoC, equity multiple, DSCR), value-add scenarios, 1-page summary memo.

| Tier | Scope | Launch | Established |
|---|---|---|---|
| Quick Screen | Template-based, 1 asset, base case | $250 | $400–500 |
| Full Underwrite | Custom model, stress cases, sensitivity tables | $600 | $1,000–1,500 |
| Institutional | Value-add/JV waterfall, promote, investor deck | $1,500 | $2,500–3,500 |

## 2. BPO / Broker Price Opinion
Desktop valuation — comp pulls, condition adjustments, ARV, exit strategy, grid.

| Tier | Scope | Launch | Established |
|---|---|---|---|
| Desktop BPO | SFR/small MF, 3-5 comps, value range | $75 | $125–175 |
| BPO + Strategy | Full grid, repair estimate, rent vs. flip | $150 | $250–350 |

**Note:** The $75 Desktop BPO is the loss-leader — fastest way to bank reviews.
Push volume here in week one.

## 3. Market Analysis Report
Submarket scorecard — rent trends, absorption, job/population growth, competitive
supply, go/no-go verdict. Maps directly to the `/market-research` skill output.

| Tier | Scope | Launch | Established |
|---|---|---|---|
| Snapshot | 1 submarket, 5 metrics, 1-page | $150 | $250–350 |
| Deep Dive | Full scorecard, comps, supply pipeline, charts | $400 | $600–900 |

## Hourly fallback
Custom/ongoing work: **$85/hr launch → $125–150/hr** after reviews + Top Rated badge.

## Launch math
The AIOS produces ~80% of each deliverable. A market analysis that takes a competitor
6 hours takes Brian ~30 min with `/market-research`. At $150 he's effectively billing
$300/hr of his time while underpricing the market — fast reviews, real margin.
