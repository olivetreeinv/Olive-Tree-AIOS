Why 641 Powder Springs St?

This is a 4–6 year opportunity to invest in a value-add, well-located apartment community with considerable upside through a clear renovation strategy.

Built in the 1960's, the offering consists of 14 units. The asset is a class B-, garden-style community offering 1-bedroom, 1-bathroom apartments. Located in the highly desirable city of Smyrna, GA — providing easy access to major employment centers and within a 15-minute commute to Truist Park.

Property Highlights

Description

Unit Mix

Renovation Scope

We will address must-haves across all 14 units: washer/dryers in-unit, kitchen backsplash, updated electric and plumbing fixtures, ceiling fans, kitchen lighting, vinyl plank flooring, new counters, cabinets, appliances, and more.

Business Plan Overview

Renovate and Sell

This presents an opportunity to acquire an apartment in a high-growth area like Smyrna. We will renovate 14 units and sell at or around year 6 after renovation and stabilization.

Financial Strategy

Investor Returns

Exit Strategy

Holding Period
