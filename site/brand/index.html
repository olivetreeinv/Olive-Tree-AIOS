01 — Brand Story

Capital, cultivated
like an orchard.

The olive tree is the oldest symbol of patient, generational wealth. It grows slowly, weathers everything, and gives back for centuries. That is the promise behind our name and our mark.

Our identity speaks to four audiences at once — private clients, institutions, everyday investors, and founders seeking capital — with one consistent voice: grounded, deliberate, and quietly confident.

02 — The Logo

The logo suite

Three forms serve every context. Always use the supplied artwork — never redraw, retype, or rearrange the elements.

Primary Lockup

Mark + wordmark, stacked

The default for most applications — websites, documents, decks, and signage. Give it room to breathe and lead with it wherever the brand needs full authority.

The Symbol

For avatars, favicons, app icons, and any square format. Use whenever the wordmark would be too small to read.

The Wordmark

For horizontal locks and tight headers where the symbol already appears nearby. Never stretch or re-space the letters.

03 — Clear Space & Sizing

Give the mark air

Maintain minimum clear space equal to one quarter of the mark's height (¼X) on every side. Keep type, imagery, and edges outside this zone.

Minimum sizes
Print · 24 mm wide

04 — Misuse

Protect the mark

Consistency is the whole point. These are the things never to do.

✕Don't stretch or distort
✕Don't rotate or tilt
✕Don't recolor off-palette
✕Don't add shadows or effects
✕Don't reduce opacity as a logo
✕Don't place on low-contrast color
